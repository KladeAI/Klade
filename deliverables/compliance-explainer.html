Clay by Klade AI — Compliance & Security Overview

AI-powered financial analysis built for regulated firms.

Data Sources: Public & Government Only

Clay's intelligence is built exclusively on publicly available, government-published data:

  • SEC EDGAR — Corporate filings (10-K, 10-Q, 8-K, proxy)
  • FRED — Federal Reserve economic indicators
  • FDIC — Bank financial & regulatory data
  • U.S. Treasury — Rates, auctions, fiscal reports

No proprietary client data is ingested, processed, or stored on Klade servers in our standard deployment.

Infrastructure & Compliance

  • SOC 2 Type II compliant infrastructure (Supabase Pro)
  • Enterprise-grade encryption at rest and in transit
  • Role-based access controls & audit logging
  • No client PII on our servers in standard deployment

Not Investment Advice

Clay is a research and analysis tool — not a financial advisor. Clay does not make trading recommendations, provide buy/sell signals, or suggest transactions. All output supports — never replaces — professional judgment.

On-Premise Deployment

For security-conscious firms requiring full data sovereignty:

  • Clay deploys entirely within the client's infrastructure
  • Client proprietary data never leaves their network
  • Same analytical capabilities, zero external exposure
  • Ideal for firms with strict compliance mandates

Architecture: Separation by Design

LayerData TypeLocation
0Raw government/public APIsKlade Cloud
1Structured & indexed dataKlade Cloud
2Enriched & vectorized analysisKlade Cloud
3Client proprietary dataOn-prem only

Layers 0–2 contain exclusively public data. Layer 3 exists only in on-premise deployments, fully isolated from Klade's cloud.

Citation-First Approach

Every data point and claim includes a traceable citation — filing name, date, section, or data series. If data is unavailable or stale, Clay discloses that explicitly. No hallucinated numbers. No uncited claims.
