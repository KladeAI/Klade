How it works: Type any command below into your Clay chat. Commands are case-insensitive -- /Brief, /brief, and /BRIEF all work the same. You can also just talk to Clay in plain English for anything not covered here.

Command Details

/brief -- Morning Market Brief

Get a complete morning briefing: major indices, key indicators, overnight movers, sector performance, economic calendar, and alerts on your watchlist stocks.

You type: /brief Clay returns: Index snapshot (S&P 500, NASDAQ, Dow) Key indicators (10Y yield, VIX) Overnight movers with reasons Sector leaders and laggards Today's economic calendar Alerts on your watchlist stocks

Options: /brief detailed for an expanded version with more depth.

/watchlist -- Manage Your Watchlist

Your watchlist powers personalized alerts and briefings. Add the companies you care about most.

/watchlist View your current watchlist with prices /watchlist add AAPL Add Apple to your watchlist /watchlist add MSFT GOOGL Add multiple tickers at once /watchlist remove TSLA Remove Tesla from your watchlist

/analyze -- Company Snapshot

A one-page snapshot of any public company: price, market cap, key financials, business description, recent developments, and Clay's take.

/analyze AAPL Snapshot of Apple /analyze NVDA Snapshot of NVIDIA /analyze JPM Snapshot of JPMorgan

Every number comes with a source citation (filing name, date, section).

/compare -- Side-by-Side Comparison

Compare 2 to 4 companies head-to-head on price, valuation, growth, margins, and returns. Clay highlights the key differences.

/compare AAPL MSFT Two companies /compare AAPL MSFT GOOGL Three companies /compare JPM BAC GS MS Four companies (max)

/earnings -- Earnings Summary

The latest earnings release for any company: revenue and EPS beat/miss, key metrics, guidance updates, call highlights, and market reaction.

/earnings AAPL Latest Apple earnings /earnings TSLA Q4 Specific quarter

Beat/miss indicators make it easy to scan: green check for beats, red X for misses.

/macro -- Macro Environment

A snapshot of the current macro landscape: Fed funds rate, Treasury yields, inflation readings, employment data, GDP, and upcoming releases.

/macro Full macro snapshot /macro rates Focus on interest rates /macro inflation Focus on inflation data /macro employment Focus on jobs data

/search -- Search Filings and Data

Search across SEC filings, earnings transcripts, and Clay's knowledge base for specific topics, disclosures, or data points.

/search AAPL revenue recognition /search "climate risk" energy sector /search JPM credit loss provisions

Results include ranked excerpts with source citations and links.

/report -- Full PDF Research Report FLAGSHIP

Clay's most comprehensive deliverable. Generates a multi-page, publication-quality PDF research report with full financial analysis.

/report AAPL Full research report on Apple /report GOOGL detailed Extended version with extra depth

Note: Reports take 1-2 minutes to generate. Clay will let you know when it's ready and deliver the PDF directly to your chat.

Report includes: Executive Summary, Business Overview, Financial Analysis, Valuation, Risk Factors, and Appendix.

/help -- Command Reference

Quick reference for all commands, or detailed help for a specific one.

/help Show all commands /help analyze Detailed help for /analyze /help compare Detailed help for /compare

Tips for Power Users

TipDetails
Mix commands and conversationStart with /analyze AAPL, then follow up in plain English: "What are the bear case risks?"
Case doesn't matter/BRIEF, /Brief, /brief all work. Tickers too: aapl becomes AAPL automatically.
Chain commandsUse /analyze for a quick look, then /report if you want the full deep dive.
Build your watchlist firstYour watchlist powers /brief alerts. Add your core positions with /watchlist add.
Ask for any formatAfter any command output, ask Clay to reformat: "Turn that into an email" or "Make it a table."
