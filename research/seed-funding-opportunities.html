Dorm Room Fund [TOP PICK]

  • Checks: $20K–$50K | Terms: Standard SAFE
  • Speed: 2–4 weeks — student investors make decisions quickly
  • Thesis: Current students or recent grads. Backed by First Round Capital. Has a Philadelphia regional team.
  • https://www.dormroomfund.com

Hustle Fund

  • Checks: $50K–$250K (sweet spot $150K) | Terms: SAFE

Precursor Ventures

  • Checks: $250K–$500K | Terms: SAFE

Z Fellows

Section 3: Non-Dilutive Grants

Ben Franklin Technology Partners SEP [TOP PICK]

  • Amount: $25K–$250K | Equity: None (payback from revenue)
  • Requirements: Tech-focused, located in Philadelphia metro
  • Sub-programs: StartupPHL Seed Fund II ($5M fund for Philly tech), General early-stage investment
